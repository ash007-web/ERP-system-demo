Understanding Correlation in ERP

What is Correlation?

Correlation measures the statistical relationship between two variables in the ERP system.

Interpretation Guide:

  • 0.7 to 1.0 Strong Positive Correlation
  • 0.3 to 0.7 Moderate Positive Correlation
  • 0 to 0.3 Weak Positive Correlation
  • -1.0 to 0 Negative Correlation

Business Insights:

Correlation analysis helps identify relationships like:

  • How sales affect inventory levels
  • Impact of attendance on productivity
  • Relationship between sales and revenue
